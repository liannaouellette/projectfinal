Mixed Greens Salad











INGREDIENTS

For the Salad: 1 cup mixed salad greens, cup
Cherry tomatoes halved, cup Cucumber sliced, Red
onion thinly sliced, Avocado sliced, Feta cheese
crumbled (optional)

For Grilled Shrimp: 6 Shrimp peeled and deveined,
Olive oil, Garlic minced, Lemon juice, Paprika,
Salt and pepper to taste

For the Dressing: Olive oil, Balsamic vinegar,
Dijon mustard, Honey, Salt and pepper to taste









Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     5-10 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
20-30 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red
onion, avocado, and feta cheese.

Step 2

In a bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, paprika, salt,
and pepper. Toss the shrimp in the marinade and let it sit for about
15 minutes. Grill the shrimp until they are cooked through, usually
2-3 minutes per side.

Step 3

In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon
mustard, honey, salt, and pepper.

Step 4

Arrange the grilled shrimp on top of the mixed greens. Drizzle the
dressing over the salad.

Step 5

Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients and serve immediately.
