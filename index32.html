Eggs With Spinach, Onions, And Tomatoes










INGREDIENTS

3 eggs

1 cup fresh spinach leaves, chopped

1/4 cup onions, diced

1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Salt and pepper to taste










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes     5-10 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
10-20 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preparation:

Whisk the eggs in a bowl until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.

Chop the spinach leaves, dice the onions, and halve the cherry tomatoes.

Step 2

Cooking:

Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

Add diced onions and sauté until translucent.

Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
Set aside.

Add cherry tomatoes and cook for a couple of minutes until they start to soften.

Step 3

Making the Omelette:

Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them set slightly.
Gently stir and fold the eggs until they are cooked to your desired consistency.

Add the sautéed spinach, tomatoes, and onions to the scrambled eggs
in the skillet. Stir gently to combine and heat the mixture for a
minute or two until everything is warmed through.

Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
