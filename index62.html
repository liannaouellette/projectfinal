Grilled Veggie Pasta








INGREDIENTS

1 cup whole-grain pasta

1 zucchini, sliced

1 eggplant, sliced

1 cup cherry tomatoes

2-3 cloves garlic, minced

2-3 tablespoons olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh basil leaves

Grated Parmesan cheese







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Boil the whole-grain pasta in salted water according to package
instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

Preheat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Drizzle olive oil
over the sliced zucchini, eggplant, and cherry tomatoes. Grill the
vegetables until they have grill marks and are slightly tender, usually
3-5 minutes per side for zucchini and eggplant, and just a few minutes
for cherry tomatoes. Remove from the grill and set aside.

Step 3

In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut
until fragrant but not browned.

Step 4

Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the olive oil-garlic sauce. Toss
in the grilled vegetables. Mix until the pasta and veggies are evenly
coated with the sauce.

Step 5

Season with salt and pepper according to taste. Optionally, garnish
with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese.
