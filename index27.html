Cranberry Oatmeal







INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup rolled oats

  • 2 cups almond milk

  • 1 teaspoon orange zest

  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds

  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup

  • 4 tablespoons homemade
    cranberry sauce

  • Mint leaves for garnishing








Prep Time:    Cook Time:
5-10 minutes      5-10 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
10-20 minutes       1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Place the rolled oats, almond milk, orange zest, chia seed and maple
in a medium pot over medium-high heat.

Step 2

Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until fully cooked.

Step 3

In a medium jar or bowl, layer the cranberry sauce and oatmeal.

Step 4

Garnish with mint leaves. Enjoy!.
