Mediterranean Chickpea Salad










INGREDIENTS

1 cup cooked chickpeas

1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

1 cucumber, diced

1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

1/3 cup olives, sliced

1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

Salt and pepper to taste

Lemon-Herb Vinaigrette Ingredients: 1/4 cup extra
virgin olive oil, 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice,
1 clove garlic, minced, 1 teaspoon dried oregano,
1 teaspoon dried basil, Salt and pepper to taste







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes       N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
20-25 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook chickpeas if not already cooked. Drain and rinse canned chickpeas.
Halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, slice the red onion thinly,
chop the olives, and chop the fresh parsley.

Step 2

In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic,
dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper to create the vinaigrette.

Step 3

In a large mixing bowl, combine the chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, diced
cucumber, sliced red onion, olives, and chopped parsley.

Step 4

Drizzle the lemon-herb vinaigrette over the salad and gently toss
until all ingredients are coated.

Step 5

Season with additional salt and pepper if needed. Serve chilled
or at room temperature.
