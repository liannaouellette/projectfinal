Baked Stuffed Potato











INGREDIENTS

1 large baking potato

1/2 cup cooked black beans

3-4 ounces lean ground beef

1/4 cup corn kernels

2 tablespoons salsa

2 tablespoons shredded cheese

Olive oil or butter

Seasonings for the ground beef

Optional toppings: Sour cream, guacamole, chopped cilantro

Pinch of salt

Butter or oil for cooking







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     45-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
60-80 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

Wash the potato thoroughly and pat it dry. Pierce the potato in several
places with a fork. Brush the potato with olive oil or melted butter and
sprinkle with salt. Place the potato directly on the oven rack and bake
for about 45-60 minutes until tender when pierced with a fork.

Step 3

In a skillet over medium heat, cook the lean ground beef until browned.
Drain excess fat. Add chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and
pepper to the cooked beef. Mix well. Stir in the cooked black beans
and corn kernels. Heat until warmed through.

Step 4

Once the baked potato is done, make a slit lengthwise and push the ends
towards the center to create an opening. Fluff up the insides with a fork.
Spoon the beef, black bean, and corn mixture into the opened potato.
Top with salsa and shredded cheese.

Step 5

Optionally, garnish with sour cream, guacamole, or chopped cilantro before serving.
