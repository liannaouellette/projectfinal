Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken











INGREDIENTS

1 large chicken breast

1 lemon

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon fresh herbs, chopped

Salt and pepper to taste

1 bunch asparagus, trimmed

1 large sweet potato, peeled and cubed

Olive oil for roasting







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     20-25 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
35-55 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

In a bowl, mix the chicken breast with lemon zest, lemon juice, minced
garlic, chopped herbs, salt, and pepper. Allow it to marinate for at
least 20-30 minutes.

Step 2

Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken
breast for about 5-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
Set aside to rest.

Step 3

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). On a baking sheet, toss the asparagus
and sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast in the oven
for 20-25 minutes or until tender and slightly browned.
