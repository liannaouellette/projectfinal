Spinach and Feta Frittata











INGREDIENTS

2 large eggs

1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: Herbs or spices of choice for seasoning

For Side Salad: 1 cup mixed salad greens, 1/4 cup pine
nuts chopped, 1/4 cup grape halved,
Light vinaigrette dressing







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
8-12 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). In a bowl, whisk the eggs together
and season with salt, pepper, and any preferred herbs or spices. Heat
olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat. Add fresh spinach
leaves to the skillet and cook until wilted. Pour the whisked eggs over
the spinach in the skillet. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese evenly over
the egg mixture. Cook the frittata on the stovetop for a few minutes
until the edges start to set.

Step 2

Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven. Bake for 10-15 minutes
or until the frittata is cooked through and slightly golden on top.

Step 3

In a bowl, toss the mixed salad greens with chopped pine nuts and
halved grapes. Dress the salad with the light vinaigrette.

Step 4

Slice the Spinach and Feta Frittata into wedges and serve alongside
the side salad.
