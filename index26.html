Baked Chicken Breast








INGREDIENTS

1 boneless, skinless chicken breast

1 medium-sized sweet potato

1 cup fresh green beans

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon paprika

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: 2 tablespoons grated cheese for added calories







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
20-30 minutes     20-30 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
40-60 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).

Step 2

Wash and peel the sweet potato. Cut it into cubes. Trim the ends of
the green beans.

Step 3

Place the chicken breast on a baking sheet. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon of
olive oil and season with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper. Flip
the chicken to coat both sides. On a separate baking sheet, toss the sweet
potato cubes and green beans with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Step 4

Place both baking sheets in the preheated oven. Bake the chicken for
about 20-25 minutes or until it reaches an internal temperature of
165°F (74°C). Bake the sweet potatoes and green beans for approximately
20-25 minutes or until tender, stirring halfway through.

Step 5

If desired, sprinkle grated cheese over the chicken breast during the last
few minutes of baking to melt and brown.

Step 6

Once cooked, serve the baked chicken breast alongside the roasted sweet
potatoes and green beans.
