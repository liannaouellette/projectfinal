Stir-Fried Shrimp








INGREDIENTS

6 ounces shrimp, peeled and deveined

1 cup mixed summer vegetables, sliced

1 cup cooked rice

2 eggs, beaten

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon sesame oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: green onions for garnish







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     5-10 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
15-25 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic
and stir briefly. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink, then remove
them from the skillet.

Step 2

In the same skillet, add the mixed summer vegetables and stir-fry until
they're slightly tender yet crisp.

Step 3

Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs
into the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked. Combine the cooked
shrimp with the vegetables and eggs in the skillet. Add cooked rice, soy
sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine and heat thoroughly.

Step 4

Garnish with green onions if desired.
