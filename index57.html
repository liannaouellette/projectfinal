Quinoa and Black Bean Bowl








INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup cooked quinoa

1/2 cup black beans

1/4 cup corn kernels

1/2 medium tomato, diced

1/4 avocado, sliced

1 tablespoon sour cream (optional)

Fresh lime wedges









Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-20 minutes     10-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Allow it to cool slightly.

Step 2

In a serving bowl, layer cooked quinoa at the base. Top it with black
beans, corn kernels, diced tomatoes, and sliced avocado.

Step 3

Add a dollop of sour cream if desired and serve with fresh lime wedges
on the side for squeezing over the bowl.
