Black Bean and Corn Tacos








INGREDIENTS

2 soft corn tortillas

1/2 cup black beans

1/2 cup corn kernels

1/4 cup diced tomatoes

1/4 cup diced red onions

1/2 teaspoon ground cumin

1/2 teaspoon chili powder

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tablespoon olive oil for sautéing

For the Avocado Slices: 1/2 ripe avocado, sliced

For the Mixed Greens: 1 cup mixed salad greens
1 tablespoon vinaigrette dressing







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
20-30 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Sauté diced onions until
translucent, then add diced tomatoes, black beans, and corn kernels.
Season with ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for
5-7 minutes until heated through.

Step 2

Slice the avocado and set it aside for serving.

Step 3

Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few
seconds. Spoon the black bean and corn filling onto the tortillas.
Top each taco with avocado slices.

Step 4

Toss the mixed salad greens with vinaigrette dressing in a separate bowl.

Step 5

Serve the Black Bean and Corn Tacos with a side of mixed greens.
