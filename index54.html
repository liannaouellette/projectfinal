Grilled Chicken + Salad








INGREDIENTS

For Grilled Chicken and Portobello Mushrooms: 1 Chicken
breasts or thighs, cup of Portobello mushrooms, 1
tablespoon Olive oil, 1 piece of Garlic minced, Lemon
juice, Salt and pepper to taste, Fresh herbs

For Roasted Sweet Potatoes: 1 Sweet potatoes peeled
and cubed, 1 tablespoon Olive oil, Salt and pepper to
taste, Optional: Smoked paprika or cinnamon for
added flavor

For Mixed Greens Salad: Mixed salad greens, cup
Cherry tomatoes halved, cup Cucumber sliced, Red
onion thinly sliced, Balsamic vinaigrette dressing









Prep Time:      Cook Time:
20-30 minutes     25-30 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
45-60 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the grill and oven to the desired temperature. Marinate the
chicken in olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and fresh
herbs. Grill the chicken and portobello mushrooms until fully cooked. Set aside.

Step 2

Toss sweet potato cubes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and optional
spices. Roast in the oven until tender and slightly crispy.

Step 3

Assemble the mixed greens salad by combining salad greens, cherry tomatoes,
cucumber, and red onion. Toss with balsamic vinaigrette.

Step 4

Plate the grilled chicken and portobello mushrooms alongside the roasted
sweet potatoes and mixed greens salad.
