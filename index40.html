Spinach and Mushroom Pasta








INGREDIENTS

1 cup whole-grain pasta

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

1 cup sliced mushrooms

2 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 cup heavy cream

2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Red pepper flakes

Salt and pepper to taste







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook the whole-grain pasta in a pot of boiling salted water according
to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside,
reserving a bit of pasta water.

Step 2

Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté
until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes. Add sliced mushrooms to the pan and
cook until they start to soften, approximately 3-4 minutes. Toss in
fresh spinach leaves and cook until they wilt down.

Step 3

Pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine with the mushroom and
spinach mixture. Add grated Parmesan cheese and continue to stir
until the sauce starts to thicken slightly. If the sauce is too thick,
you can add a splash of reserved pasta water to adjust the consistency.

Step 4

Add the cooked whole-grain pasta to the pan with the creamy spinach
and mushroom sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is evenly
coated with the sauce.

Step 5

Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using), adjusting
to taste. Serve the creamy pasta hot, garnishing with additional
grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
