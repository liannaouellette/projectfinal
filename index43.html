Caprese Salad








INGREDIENTS

6 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

1 large tomatoes, sliced

Fresh basil leaves

1/2 ripe avocado, sliced

Balsamic glaze

Salt and pepper to taste











Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes        N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
5-10 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Slice the fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, basil leaves, and avocado.

Step 2

Arrange the mozzarella slices, tomato slices, and avocado alternately
on a serving plate or platter. Add fresh basil leaves between the layers.

Step 3

Drizzle the assembled salad with balsamic glaze.

Step 4

Season with salt and pepper to taste.
