Chicken Caesar Salad








INGREDIENTS

4-6 oz Grilled Chicken Breast

2 cups Romaine Lettuce, chopped

1/2 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved

1/4 cup Croutons

2 tablespoons Caesar Dressing












Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Season chicken breast with preferred seasonings and grill until fully
cooked. Slice or chop it for the salad.

Step 2

In a large bowl, combine chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes,
croutons, and the grilled chicken.

Step 3

Drizzle Caesar dressing over the salad just before serving. Toss the
salad gently to coat evenly with the dressing.
