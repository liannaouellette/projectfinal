Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast











INGREDIENTS

1 boneless, skinless chicken breast

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Mixed salad greens

Cherry tomatoes

Balsamic vinaigrette or dressing of choice







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
20-30 minutes     20-30 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
40-60 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2

Place the chicken breast between plastic wrap and gently pound it to an
even thickness. Season both sides of the chicken breast with salt and pepper.

Step 3

In a bowl, mix the fresh spinach leaves with crumbled feta cheese.

Step 4

Carefully slice a pocket into the side of the chicken breast. Stuff the
pocket with the spinach and feta mixture and secure with toothpicks
if needed.

Step 5

Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the stuffed chicken breast. Place it
on a baking sheet or oven-safe dish and bake for about 20-25 minutes
or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal
temperature of 165°F (74°C).

Step 6

While the chicken is baking, assemble a side salad using mixed greens
and cherry tomatoes. Dress the salad with balsamic vinaigrette or your
preferred dressing.
