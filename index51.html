Whole Grain Pancakes with Fruit











INGREDIENTS

1 cup whole grain pancake mix

Water or milk

Cooking spray or butter for the pan

Sliced fruits

Greek yogurt (optional)

Maple syrup or honey (optional)










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
15-25 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Follow the instructions on the pancake mix package to prepare the batter.
Typically, you'll mix the pancake mix with water or milk until it reaches
the desired consistency.

Step 2

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly coat
it with cooking spray or butter. Pour the pancake batter onto the skillet
to form pancakes of your preferred size. Cook until bubbles form on the
surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

Step 3

Once the pancakes are cooked, arrange the sliced fruits (pineapple,
kiwi, berries, etc.) on top of the pancakes. Add a dollop of Greek
yogurt if desired.

Step 4

Optionally, drizzle maple syrup or honey over the pancakes and fruit.
