Nutritious Smoothie








INGREDIENTS

1 ripe banana

1 cup frozen mixed berries

1 cup fresh spinach leaves

1 cup 2% milk

1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup

Optional: 1-2 tablespoons nut butter or protein
powder for extra calories and protein











Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes      
Total Time:      Servings:
5-10 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Peel the ripe banana and break it into chunks. Measure out the frozen
mixed berries and fresh spinach leaves.

Step 2

In a blender, add the banana chunks, frozen mixed berries, fresh
spinach leaves, and 2% milk. Optionally, add honey or maple syrup
for sweetness, and nut butter or protein powder for additional calories
and protein. Blend all the ingredients until smooth and well combined.
If the smoothie is too thick, add more milk to reach your desired consistency.

Step 3

Pour the blended smoothie into a glass or bowl.
