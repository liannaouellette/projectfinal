Vegetable Curry








INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup seasonal chickpeas (precooked or canned)

1 cup mixed seasonal vegetables, chopped

1/2 onion, diced

1 small piece of ginger, grated

1/2 cup coconut milk

1 tablespoon olive oil or coconut oil

1 tablespoon curry powder or your preferred curry spices

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: Red pepper flakes or chili powder for added heat

Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

For the Brown Rice: 1/2 cup brown rice, 1 cup water
or vegetable broth







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     25-35 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
40-55 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Rinse the brown rice thoroughly. In a pot, bring water or vegetable
broth to a boil. Add the rice, reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer
for 35-45 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
Set aside.

Step 2

Heat olive oil or coconut oil in a pan over medium heat. Add diced onions
and sauté until translucent. Stir in minced garlic and grated ginger, and
cook for another minute until fragrant. Add the mixed seasonal vegetables
and chickpeas to the pan, and sauté for a few minutes until slightly tender.
Sprinkle curry powder or your preferred curry spices over the vegetables
and chickpeas. Stir to coat evenly. Pour in the coconut milk, reduce the
heat, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until the
vegetables are cooked to your liking. Season with salt, pepper, and optional
red pepper flakes or chili powder. Adjust seasoning to taste.

Step 3

Serve the vegetable curry over the cooked brown rice. Garnish with fresh
cilantro if desired.
