Vegetable Stir-Fry













INGREDIENTS

1 cup cooked brown rice

4 ounces chicken breast, sliced

1 cup mixed vegetables

2 tablespoons olive oil or sesame oil

2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari

1 tablespoon hoisin sauce

1 clove garlic, minced

1/2 teaspoon grated ginger

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: 2 tablespoons chopped nuts or seeds







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
20-30 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook the brown rice according to package instructions. Set aside.

Step 2

Slice the chicken breast into thin strips. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in
a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the sliced chicken and cook until
it's fully cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 3

In the same skillet, add the remaining oil. Add minced garlic and grated
ginger, and saut for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the mixed
vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they
are tender yet crisp.

Step 4

Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Add
soy sauce (or tamari) and hoisin sauce (if using). Toss everything
together until well combined and heated through.

Step 5

Divide the cooked brown rice into a bowl or plate. Top it
with the chicken and vegetable stir-fry.
