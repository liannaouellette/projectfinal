Overnight Oats












INGREDIENTS

1/2 cup rolled oats

1 cup almond milk (unsweetened)

1 tablespoon chia seeds

1/2 cup diced peaches

1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup

2 tablespoons chopped nuts










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
5-10 minutes      N/A
Total Time:      Servings:
5-10 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

In a mason jar or airtight container, add rolled oats, almond milk,
chia seeds, diced peaches, and sweetener (if using). Stir the ingredients
well until thoroughly combined.

Step 2

Cover the jar/container and refrigerate it overnight or for at least
4-6 hours to allow the oats and chia seeds to absorb the liquid and soften.

Step 3

Before serving, give the mixture a good stir. Top it with chopped nuts
for added calories and a crunchy texture.
