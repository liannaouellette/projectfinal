Vegetable Burger








INGREDIENTS

4 oz ground beef

1 hamburger bun

1 slice cheese

Lettuce leaves

Sliced tomatoes

Sliced onions

Ketchup, mustard, or preferred condiments

For Sweet Potato Fries: 1 medium sweet potato,
1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste,
Optional: Seasonings like paprika, garlic powder,
or chili powder

For Steamed Green Beans: 1 cup fresh green beans
trimmed, Salt to taste







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Season the ground beef with salt and pepper and shape it into a patty.
Cook the beef patty on a grill or in a skillet over medium-high heat
until it reaches your desired doneness. Toast the hamburger bun lightly
if preferred. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato slices, onion
slices, cheese, and condiments.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 425F (220C). Cut the sweet potato into thin
strips to resemble fries. Toss the sweet potato strips with olive oil,
salt, pepper, and any desired seasonings. Spread them on a baking sheet
and bake for 20-25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden
brown and crispy.

Step 3

Place the green beans in a steamer basket over boiling water. Cover and
steam for about 4-5 minutes or until tender yet crisp. Season with salt.

Step 4

Plate the beef burger on the bun with all the fixings. Serve with a portion
of sweet potato fries and a side of steamed green beans.
