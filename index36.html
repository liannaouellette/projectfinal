Baked Salmon








INGREDIENTS

6-8 oz salmon fillet

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: Lemon slices or herbs for garnish

For Roasted Winter Squash: 1 cup cubed winter squash,
1 tablespoon olive oil, Salt, pepper, and
any preferred seasonings

For Quinoa: 1/2 cup quinoa, 1 cup water or vegetable broth









Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     25-30 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
40-50 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Drizzle the salmon with olive oil and season it generously with salt
and pepper. Optionally, add lemon slices or herbs on top of the salmon.

Step 3

Toss the cubed winter squash with olive oil, salt, pepper, and any
preferred seasonings. Spread the squash on a separate baking sheet
lined with parchment paper.

Step 4

Place both baking sheets in the preheated oven. Bake the salmon for
about 12-15 minutes or until it flakes easily with a fork. Roast the
squash for approximately 20-25 minutes or until tender and golden.

Step 5

Rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a saucepan, bring water or
vegetable broth to a boil. Add the quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover,
and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the liquid is absorbed and
the quinoa is cooked.

Step 6

Plate the baked salmon alongside the roasted winter squash and
a serving of cooked quinoa.
