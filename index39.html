Bean and Vegetable Chili












INGREDIENTS

4 oz lean ground beef

1/2 cup cooked beans

1/2 cup diced squash

1/4 cup diced onions

1/4 cup diced bell peppers

1/2 cup crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce

1 cup beef or vegetable broth

1 tablespoon chili powder

1 teaspoon cumin powder

Salt and pepper to taste

1 tablespoon olive oil

Optional: Chopped fresh cilantro for garnish

For Toppings: 2 tablespoons shredded cheese,
2 tablespoons sour cream

For Quinoa: 1/2 cup cooked quinoa




Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     45-60 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
60-80 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add diced onions and bell
peppers, sauté until softened. Add lean ground beef, break it up with a
spoon, and cook until browned. Stir in diced squash, chili powder, cumin
powder, salt, and pepper. Pour in crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce and
beef or vegetable broth. Add cooked beans and bring the mixture to a
simmer. Let it cook for 20-25 minutes until the flavors meld and the
squash is tender. Adjust seasoning if needed.

Step 2

Prepare quinoa according to package instructions.

Step 3

Place a serving of the bean and vegetable chili in a bowl. Top it
with shredded cheese, a dollop of sour cream, and optionally garnish
with chopped fresh cilantro. Serve it alongside a small portion
of cooked quinoa.
