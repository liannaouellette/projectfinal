Grilled Fish with Veggies








INGREDIENTS

4 ounces of White fish filets

Olive oil 1 tablespoon

Lemon juice using 1 lemon

Garlic, minced 1 clove

Dried herbs

Salt and pepper to taste

For Roasted Summer Squash: 1 Summer squash sliced,
Olive oil, Salt and pepper to taste, Optional:
Garlic powder, onion powder, or herbs for additional flavor

For Couscous: 1 cup of Couscous, 1 cup Chicken or
vegetable broth, Olive oil 1 tablespoon, Salt and pepper
to taste, Optional: Chopped fresh herbs for garnish







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
20-30 minutes     30-35 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
50-65 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried herbs, salt,
and pepper. Marinate the fish filets in this mixture for about 15-30
minutes. Grill the fish until cooked through, usually 3-4 minutes per side.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss sliced summer squash with olive
oil, salt, pepper, and optional seasonings. Roast in the oven for about
20-25 minutes or until tender, turning halfway through.

Step 3

Cook couscous according to package instructions, using broth instead of
water for added flavor. Fluff the cooked couscous with a fork and season
with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired.

Step 4

Arrange the grilled fish on a plate. Serve with roasted summer squash
on one side and a portion of couscous on the other.
