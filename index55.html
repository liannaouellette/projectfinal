Baked Cod with Roasted Vegetables











INGREDIENTS

1 cod fillet

1 small zucchini, sliced

1-2 carrots, peeled and sliced

1 bell pepper, sliced

Olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Optional: Seasonings like garlic powder, paprika, or herbs

1/2 cup cooked jasmine rice







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     20-25 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400F (200C).

Step 2

On a baking sheet, place the sliced zucchini, carrots, and bell pepper.
Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and any desired
seasonings. Toss to coat evenly.

Step 3

Roast the vegetables in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or
until they are tender and slightly browned, stirring occasionally.

Step 4

Place the cod fillet on another baking sheet or in an oven-safe dish.
Drizzle with a little olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and any
desired seasonings.

Step 5

Place the cod in the oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until
the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

Step 6

Prepare 1/2 cup of jasmine rice according to package instructions.

Step 7

Place the cooked jasmine rice on a plate. Arrange the baked cod
fillet on top of the rice. Serve the roasted vegetables alongside
the fish and rice.
