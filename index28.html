Veggie Pasta Salad








INGREDIENTS

1 cup cooked pasta (whole-grain)

Assorted mixed vegetables diced or sliced

2/4 cup chopped walnuts

For the vinaigrette: 1 tablespoon olive oil,
1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard,
Herbs and spices to taste












Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Once cooked,
drain and let it cool.

Step 2

Dice or slice the assorted mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
Chop the walnuts into smaller pieces.

Step 3

In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, Dijon mustard,
and your choice of herbs and spices. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

Step 4

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled pasta with the
assorted chopped vegetables and chopped walnuts. Pour the prepared
vinaigrette over the pasta, vegetables, and walnuts. Gently toss
the salad until the ingredients are evenly coated with the vinaigrette.
