Salmon with Roasted Asparagus








INGREDIENTS

4-6 oz Salmon Fillet

Olive Oil

Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Lemon slices (optional)

Herbs

For Roasted Asparagus: 8-10 Asparagus spears,
Olive Oil, Salt and Pepper (to taste)

For Quinoa: 1/4 cup Quinoa, 1/2 cup Water or Broth,
Salt (to taste)







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
10-15 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
20-30- minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375F (190C).

Step 2

Place the salmon fillet on a baking sheet. Drizzle olive oil and season
with salt, pepper, and herbs. Place lemon slices on top if desired.

Step 3

Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes or until it flakes easily with a fork.

Step 4

Trim the woody ends of the asparagus.

Step 5

Place the asparagus on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle
salt and pepper.

Step 6

Roast in the oven at 375F (190C) for about 10-12 minutes or until tender.

Step 7

Rinse the quinoa thoroughly in a fine-mesh sieve.

Step 8

In a pot, combine quinoa, water or broth, and salt. Bring to a boil, then
reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes. Let it sit for 5 minutes
before fluffing with a fork.
