Spring Vegetable Frittata











INGREDIENTS

3 large Eggs

1/4 cup Bell Peppers (diced)

1/4 cup Cherry Tomatoes (halved)

1/4 cup Spinach (chopped)

1/4 cup Onion (diced)

Salt and Pepper (to taste)

1 tablespoon Olive Oil

For Side Salad: Mixed Greens, Cucumber (sliced)
Balsamic Vinaigrette







Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     15-20 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
30-40 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a small oven-safe skillet.

Step 2

In a skillet, heat olive oil. Sauté bell peppers, cherry tomatoes,
spinach, and onions until slightly softened.

Step 3

In a bowl, beat the eggs, season with salt and pepper. Pour over
the sautéed vegetables.

Step 4

Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 10-12
minutes until the eggs are set.

Step 5

Mix your choice of mixed greens and sliced cucumbers in a bowl.

Step 6

Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette or dressing of your preference over the salad.
