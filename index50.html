Grilled Vegetable Skewers








INGREDIENTS

1 cup quinoa, uncooked

1 block of halloumi cheese OR firm tofu, cubed

Assorted vegetables for skewers

Olive oil for brushing

Salt and pepper to taste

Marinade for halloumi or tofu










Prep Time:      Cook Time:
15-20 minutes     10-15 minutes
Total Time:      Servings:
25-35 minutes     1 serving

Directions

Step 1

Cook quinoa according to package instructions.

Step 2

If using tofu, press it to remove excess water and cut it into cubes.
If using halloumi, cut it into cubes. In a bowl, prepare a marinade
for the halloumi or tofu by combining olive oil, lemon juice, minced
garlic, and herbs. Marinate the halloumi or tofu for at least 15-30
minutes. Preheat the grill or grill pan over medium-high heat.

Step 3

Thread the marinated halloumi or tofu cubes onto skewers alternating
with the assorted vegetables. Brush the skewers with olive oil and
season with salt and pepper. Grill the skewers for about 10-15 minutes,
turning occasionally, until the vegetables are charred and the halloumi
or tofu is grilled.

Step 4

Serve the grilled vegetable skewers over cooked quinoa.
